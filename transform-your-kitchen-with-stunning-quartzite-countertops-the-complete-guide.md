## **What Is Quartzite? An Introduction to This Elegant Natural Stone**

### **Understanding Quartzite – Nature’s Marvel**

Quartzite is a natural stone formed under extreme pressure and heat from
sandstone deposits. Over millions of years, these deposits transform into a
dense, durable, and visually striking material. Many confuse quartzite with
quartz, but they are actually entirely different materials, each with unique
characteristics.

### **The Composition of Quartzite**

Unlike quartz, which is engineered, quartzite is completely natural and
primarily composed of silica. Its crystalline structure gives it remarkable
hardness, making it an excellent choice for countertops. Curious about its
origins? Read more on Wikipedia’s
[Quartzite](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Quartzite) page for an in-depth
scientific overview.

## **Maintenance and Longevity of Quartzite Countertops**

### **Cleaning Tips**

Use mild soap and warm water for everyday cleaning. Avoid harsh chemicals or
acidic cleaners, which can etch the surface.

### **Sealing Your Quartzite**

Sealing is vital to safeguard against stains. We recommend resealing every
6-12 months, depending on use.
