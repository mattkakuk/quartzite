## **What’s the Difference Between Granite and Quartzite?**

Let’s clear up the confusion fast—because a lot of people mix up stone names
when they’re researching.

### **Granite Is an Igneous Natural Stone**

Granite typically has a speckled or granular look, with crystals and mineral
blends that create depth and sparkle.

### **Quartzite Is a Metamorphic Natural Stone**

Quartzite often has flowing veining and a more “sweeping” visual—especially
the popular white and gray styles.

#### **The Best Way to Think About It**

Granite is like a well-made pickup truck—dependable, versatile, and built to
work. Quartzite is like a luxury SUV—still tough, but with that premium, head-
turning style.
