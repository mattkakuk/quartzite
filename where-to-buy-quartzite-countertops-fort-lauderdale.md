## **Quartzite vs. Granite and Quartz—What’s Better?**

Many homeowners compare **granite and quartzite** before making a final
decision—and that’s smart.

### **Granite: The Classic Choice**

Granite is durable and timeless with speckled patterns and darker tones. It’s
a great option for traditional kitchens.

### **Quartzite: The Designer Choice**

Quartzite is typically chosen for its elegance and high-end veining. It often
resembles marble but brings more strength for real-world use.

### **Quartz (Engineered): The Low-Maintenance Option**

Quartz doesn’t require sealing, but it’s not natural stone. If you want a
unique slab with natural movement, quartzite wins.
