## **Quartzite Countertop vs Granite and Quartzite: What’s the Real
Difference?**

Let’s clear up the confusion—because this topic gets messy online.

### **Quartzite vs Granite**

Granite often has a more speckled or granular pattern, while quartzite tends
to have flowing movement and veining. Both are natural stones, and both can be
excellent depending on your design goals.

### **Quartzite vs Quartz**

Quartz is engineered (man-made), with consistent patterns. Quartzite is
natural, with unique variation.

#### **If You Love Natural Movement**

You’ll likely gravitate toward quartzite.

Finish is not a small detail—it’s a personality shift.

### **Polished Finish**

  * Bright, reflective, classic
  * Makes colors and veining pop
  * Great for a clean, luxury look

### **Honed Finish**

  * Softer, matte feel
  * More understated and modern
  * It can show wear differently depending on the stone

### **Leathered Finish**

  * Textured, warm, high-end
  * Excellent for hiding fingerprints in daily life
  * Looks incredible with natural movement stones

#### **Our Take**

If you want “designer kitchen energy” without constantly wiping smudges,
leathered finishes can be a great choice.
